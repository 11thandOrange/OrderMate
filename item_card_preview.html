🖼️ #59: Dynamic Item Icons

👆 #60: Item Row Click

Click any item row to open the ItemNoteDialogFragment popup.
Shows item name, modifiers, quantity, and widget-based note editors.

When items overflow, a bouncing chevron appears at the bottom.
Indicator hides when scrolled to bottom via canScrollVertically(1).
