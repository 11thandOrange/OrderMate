Features

Powerful Order Management Tools

OrderMate gives merchants unique calendar scheduling, customizable notes, and easy to use SMS & email notification.

Item-Level Notes

Attach custom notes to individual line items. Perfect for special requests, allergies, modifications, and per-item instructions that stay with each product.

Order-Level Notes

Add notes to the entire order for delivery addresses, pickup times, gift messages, or any information that applies to the whole transaction.

Visual Calendar

View all scheduled orders in Day, Week, or Month view. Jump to today or next fulfillment with one tap. Never miss a pickup or delivery again.

Customer Notifications

Send SMS or email notifications to customers when their order is ready. Create templates for messages you send most often!

Schedule Orders

Merchants can schedule email reminders sent to them before an order is due. And schedule receipt printing so the printers automatically print upcoming orders before one is due.

Smart Search & Filters

Customize filters to quickly search & find orders. Only search by the filters that matter most to you.

Register Overlay Integration

Access OrderMate directly from Clover Register with a floating button. Add notes and schedule orders without leaving the checkout flow.

Profile Customization

Personalize with custom avatars and theme colors. Choose from multiple accent colors to match your brand or personal preference.

Order History Tracking

View complete order history with payment details, refunds, and transaction timeline. Track partially paid, partially refunded, and closed orders.
